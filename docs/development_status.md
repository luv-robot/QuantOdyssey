## Research Principle

Strategy research is now human-led. LLM/agent components should not be treated as alpha
discoverers. A human ResearchThesis records the market observation, hypothesis, trade logic,
expected regimes, invalidation conditions, and constraints. Agents implement, test, critique,
and document the thesis.

Research direction has been clarified in:

- `docs/product_vision.md`
- `docs/research_os.md`
- `docs/evaluation_profiles.md`
- `docs/harness_researcher.md`
- `docs/execution_integration.md`

Key decisions:

- Test all priority strategy families first, then explore the relatively higher-frequency families.
- OHLCV is acceptable for L0 screening and falsification, but not for full proof of crowding or forced flow.
- Event definitions are generated by strategy family and can later be overridden by humans.
- Initial regimes are NORMAL, STRONG_TREND, CRASH, and CHAOS.
- Permission Layer is research-only for now: score context risk, do not trade, disable, or size positions.
- Low-frequency strategies with insufficient samples enter Watchlist instead of automatic rejection.
- Harness should behave like a researcher: summarize failures, request missing data, and keep the research loop moving.
- The research platform should connect to real ordering through DeploymentCandidate export and ExecutionFeedback ingestion, while keeping order routing and exchange secrets outside QuantOdyssey.
- Long-term product direction is a multi-user AI-assisted strategy research platform for hobbyist and small-team quant researchers, with Strategy Arena as a potential commercialization path.
- Core output should be a private ReviewSession: a reproducible scorecard plus an evidence-first AI research dialogue.
- Research quality should be summarized with Research Maturity Score rather than a strategy credit score or investment-readiness score.
- V1 user input scope is natural-language ResearchThesis. V2 may support Freqtrade-compatible Python file upload.
- V1 minimum experience starts with Thesis Pre-Review: structure completeness, condition clarity, commonness risk, at most three AI questions, and a Research Design Draft before strategy generation.

## External Integration Blockers

- Real Postgres persistence needs a `POSTGRES_URL`; local SQLite works for development.
- Real vector storage needs Qdrant or Chroma connection details.
- Real Freqtrade backtesting needs Freqtrade installed and historical data downloaded.
- Real LLM strategy generation needs provider API keys and model routing.
- Prefect and n8n orchestration need running services and deployment configuration.
- Binance public API access may require network permissions and may be region/IP restricted.
- Phase 9 Dashboard requires choosing and installing a UI/runtime stack such as FastAPI + React or Streamlit.
